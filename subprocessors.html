Subprocessors overview

Service categories used to deliver isops.ai, reviewed without guesswork.

The isops.ai team can route detailed diligence separately, but this page gives procurement, privacy, and security reviewers a clean starting point for the service categories commonly involved in delivering the platform.

Infrastructure and hosting

Service providers used to host and operate the application, store platform data, and support secure delivery of customer-facing workflows.

Communications and support

Service providers used for transactional email, product communication, support coordination, or buyer review routing.

AI provider category

AI generation is provider-configurable. When enabled by the operator, the configured provider supports copilot generation for remediation, evidence planning, and control operations.
