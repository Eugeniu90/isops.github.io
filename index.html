How is this different from AWS Audit Manager?

Audit Manager collects AWS evidence. isops.ai connects posture, remediation, evidence, and trust artifacts into one workflow.

Can we bring our own AI provider?

Yes. Bring your own provider for live generation. When unconfigured, the product says so honestly.

What happens if our compliance workflow data is still sparse?

Missing data is shown clearly. The platform never manufactures a clean score from sparse inputs.

Can this help customer-facing trust reviews?

Yes. Reports and evidence packages are built for customer security reviews and audit support.
