Workflow

From finding to evidence without losing the narrative.

Most platforms stop at a list of gaps. isops.ai follows the full operational path: detect, assess, remediate, validate, document, and package the output for internal review or external audit.

1. Detect the real gap

Import the latest scan, classify severity, map controls, and show why the issue matters in the context of the environment.

2. Draft the treatment plan

Create or refine remediation with CLI, Terraform, CloudFormation, AI guidance, rollback, and validation steps.

3. Attach evidence deliberately

Link findings, control expectations, and evidence artifacts so report output stays grounded in the real remediation trail.

4. Export audit-grade output

Package the real current state into executive and auditor-facing reports that explain not just what failed, but what happened next.

Give operators a copilot, not another button.

isops.ai Copilot shows up where decisions are being made: findings, remediation playbooks, CAPA, risk treatment, and evidence planning. When AI is configured, the assistant works inside the workflow. When it is not configured, the product stays honest.

FAQ

Questions security and compliance teams ask before they switch.

How is this different from AWS Audit Manager?

Audit Manager is useful for collecting AWS-oriented evidence. isops.ai is the broader operating surface that connects posture, remediation, risk treatment, evidence, and exported trust artifacts into one workflow model.

Can we bring our own AI provider?

Yes. The platform supports configured AI providers for live copilot generation, and it stays honest when no provider is configured by exposing a setup-required state instead of simulating output.

What happens if our compliance workflow data is still sparse?

isops.ai separates security posture from compliance-program maturity. If policies, evidence, CAPA, or training records are missing, the platform shows that clearly instead of manufacturing a clean score.

Can this help customer-facing trust reviews?

That is one of the design goals. The exported reports and evidence packages are meant to be usable for customer security reviews, internal readiness checks, and audit support.
