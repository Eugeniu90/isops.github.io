Category Examples Why it appears in isops.ai

Privacy review checklist

  • Validate which environments and frameworks are in scope for the evaluation.
  • Review whether exported evidence or reports will include any customer-specific identifiers.
  • Confirm whether AI provider configuration will be enabled for the walkthrough or remain disabled.
  • Route subprocessors and security follow-up through the linked trust pages.
